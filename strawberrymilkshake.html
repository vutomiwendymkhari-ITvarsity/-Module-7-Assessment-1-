Strawberry Milkshake

Prep time: 10mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients






Steps

  1. 1. Blend half of strawberries, 1/4 tsp. vanilla, 1 cup ice cream and 2 Tbsp. milk in blender until smooth.
  2. S2. Pour into 2 glasses. Repeat with remaining strawberries, vanilla, ice cream and milk. Serve immediately.
